Before any visualization you should be clear that what the data are, how they were collected and who is going to look at it.
You must be well-versed in data in order to visualize it. You should be clear that who is your audience. Is it your boss or colleague.
You should be clear that how will you convey your results . Is it a presentation, document or email.

Let us take an example of funding for free courses.

A complete sentence may look like -

This year the students who took classes from our funding performed 10% better than those students who did not take the classes.
IT is recommended that we should continue the funding to support students and it will also increase company reputation.
